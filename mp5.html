I added the navigation bar to all of my pages, made adjustments to the navigation bar to have it look the way I want it to look, and added all of the links to the bottom of my pages. After some feed back from my instructor, I was able to validate all of my pages. I learned so much from these exercises. I am so excited to get started with my final project.


When validating my pages I had started out with 7 errors, but discovered that I had put the top of the page navigation table outside of the body tags. Once I fixed that, I was left with 2 errors related  to this code:  <tbody bgcolor= "(rgba(0,0,0,1.00)" align="center"> <style type="text/css">. Turns out I had simply put the bgcolor and the style tags in the wrong places. When those 2 items were fixed, all of my pages passed validation!
