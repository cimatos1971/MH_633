Welcome to Iris Matos's Mini Project 3 Digital Audio Page.

In order to make ths audio file, I went to a website called Bensound to find open source music that I liked. I downloaded the files to my computer and then imported them into Audacity. Once in Audacity, I recorded my voice and then used the split, time slide, mix, cut, and normalize tools to finalize my project.
