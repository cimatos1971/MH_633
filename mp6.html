Video Assignment for Multimedia Class.

I decided to introduce my dogs for this assignment. I located pictures, videos, and music that I wanted to use. Then I opened up Movie Maker and imported all of the pictures. In order to do the voice over in the tool, I would have had to pay so instead I went to Audacity, built my audio file there and then imported that into the Movie Maker tool. I used the fade effects as transitions between the pictures and added text where appropriate. I then downloaded the file into the mp4 format. This project was actually a lot of fun!
